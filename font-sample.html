Mascarpone Pancakes
with Chocolate Chips & Orange Zest
by Felicia Wang
Sift together dry ingredients.
  • Flour
    3 cup
  • Baking powder
    3 tsp
  • Salt
    1/4 tsp
Beat together wet ingredients.
  • Milk
    3 cup
  • Mascarpone cheese
    1/2 cup
  • Sugar
    3 tbsp
  • Eggs
    3 large
  • Vanilla extract
    2 tsp
  • Butter
    4 tbsp
  • Orange zest
    1 orange
Stir in chocolate chips when combining wet and dry ingredients.
  • Chocolate chips
    1 cup
Heat frying pay, coat with non-stick spary. Ladel batter into pan.
Cook until golden brown on each side.
Serve with maple syrup, butter, whipped cream and orange slices.
