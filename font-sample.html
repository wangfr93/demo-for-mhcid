Mascarpone Pancakes with Chocolate Chips & Orange Zest by Felicia Wang Sift together dry ingredients. Flour (3 cups) Baking powder (3 tsp) Salt (1/4 tsp) Beat together wet ingredients. Milk (3 cups) Mascarpone (1/2 cups) Sugar (3 tbsp) Eggs (3 large) Vanilla etract (2 tsp) Butter (4 tbsp) Orange zest (1 orange) Chocolate chips (1 cup) Stir in chocolate chips when combining wet and dry ingredients. Heat frying pan, coat w/ non-stick spray. Ladel batter into pan.
